Please give me the total budget in GBP."

*   **Location:** Shinjuku, Tokyo (Central hub for transport and nightlife)

### 🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

**Day 1: Arrival & Shinjuku Settlement (Oct 16)**
*   **Afternoon:** Arrive at Tokyo Haneda (15:30). Clear immigration and take the Keikyu Line or Monorail to Shinjuku.

*   **Cost:** Transport £15 | Food £10

**Day 2: Electric Town & Anime Culture (Oct 17)**
*   **Morning:** Train to Akihabara Electric Town.
*   **Activity:** Explore Multi Anime Center and Radio Kaikan. Browse electronics and manga.
*   **Lunch:** Maid cafe experience or budget anime-themed cafe.
*   **Afternoon:** Continue exploring tech shops (Yodobashi Camera).
*   **Cost:** Activity £50 | Food £20 | Transport £10

**Day 3: Vintage Collectibles & Hidden Alleys (Oct 18)**
*   **Morning:** Travel to Nakano Broadway.
*   **Activity:** Hunt for vintage anime figures and collectibles (less crowded than Akihabara).
*   **Evening:** Return to Shinjuku. Walk through Omoide Yokocho (Memory Lane).
*   **Dinner:** Yakitori and drinks in the narrow alleyway stalls.
*   **Cost:** Activity £40 | Food/Drink £35 | Transport £10

**Day 4: Shibuya Crossing & Street Fashion (Oct 19)**
*   **Morning:** Head to Shibuya.
*   **Activity:** Experience the Shibuya Crossing and explore Center Gai.
*   **Lunch:** Street food stalls in the area.
*   **Afternoon:** Visit The Millennials Shibuya (even if not staying, observe the smart pod tech) or shop for youth fashion.
*   **Cost:** Activity £30 | Food £20 | Transport £10

**Day 5: Digital Art & Seafood Market (Oct 20)**
*   **Morning:** Travel to Toyosu for TeamLab Planets.
*   **Activity:** Immersive digital art museum (Tech focus).
*   **Afternoon:** Head to Tsukiji Outer Market.
*   **Lunch/Dinner:** Fresh sushi, tamagoyaki, and seafood skewers.
*   **Cost:** Activity £35 | Food £40 | Transport £15

**Day 6: The Ramen Quest (Oct 21)**
*   **Morning:** Free time for souvenir shopping or revisiting favorite spots.
*   **Lunch:** Ramen Nagi (Shinjuku). Try the customisable niboshi (dried sardine) ramen.
*   **Afternoon:** Relax at the hotel lounge or explore local tech vending machines.
*   **Evening:** Final budget-friendly street food hunt.
*   **Cost:** Activity £12 | Food £25 | Transport £5

**Day 7: Departure Prep (Oct 22)**
*   **Morning:** Last-minute packing. Ensure Suica card has enough credit for airport transfer.
*   **Afternoon:** Check out of 9h Nine Hours. Travel to Haneda Airport.

### 💡 Traveler Tips for Mid-October
*   **Weather:** Mid-October is pleasant (15°C - 22°C). Bring layers and a light rain jacket.
*   **Connectivity:** Rent a Pocket WiFi or buy an eSIM upon arrival at Haneda for navigation.
*   **Cash is King:** While cards are accepted in major stores, many ramen shops and street stalls in Omoide Yokocho only accept Cash (Yen).
*   **Suica Card:** Load this IC card upon arrival for seamless train travel across Tokyo.
*   **Capsule Etiquette:** At 9h Nine Hours, remember to remove shoes before entering the sleeping pod and keep noise to a minimum in the lounge areas.
